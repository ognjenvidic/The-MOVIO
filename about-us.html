About Us

Every step matters – our journey to sustainability began in the 1980s.

Since 1981 MOVIO© has delighted design enthusiasts worldwide with an endless array of innovative furniture, lighting, and decor – each piece radiating an undeniable passion for creative design.

The Mission

Redefining spaces with sustainability, fueled by innovation and quality

Our mission doesn't end at the doorstep of our customers' doors or in front of our premises.

At MOVIO© we celebrate individuality by transforming spaces into vibrant expressions of personality and style. With a passion for the extraordinary, we create and offer a wide range of very unique, imaginative and joy-filled furniture, lighting, and decor.

While our goal is ambitious, we're making meaningful progress every day, striving for continuous improvement in every aspect of our business. To further this commitment, we've launched the "Let's Do Better" initiative, where we're dedicating all our efforts to enhancing sustainability across every facet of the company.

Peter Schönhofen Founder & CEO MOVIO© Design

We believe in creating more than just furniture: we craft enduring pieces that stand the test of time. Our mission is to design timeless favorites, built with quality and care, to foster lifelong connections and bring lasting joy to your home.

Timeless Materials

Instead of mass production, we focus on small furniture series, which we produce in workshops and manufactories from high quality materials.

Wood with origin

Each manufacturer must provide us with information on the origin of the wood and other natural materials as well as submit current, recognized certificates for legal and ecologically compatible procurement.

Leather for life

Real leather is naturally resistant to abrasion, resistant and tear resistant. Especially in comparison to cheap leather alternatives, high-quality leather is more environmentally friendly when it comes to production.

Natural to the touch

Our home textiles have a good feel to them. They are crafted from natural materials such as cotton, linen or near-natural resources such as viscose, which is based on plant fibers.
